DCSS SCREEN

Map section is in top left and is 17 rows by 33 columns
Status section starts on row 1 column 38 (using 1-based indexes) and is 12(?) rows
Messages section starts on row 18 and is 6 rows long

How to tell if screen is finished drawing

a - ability screen
Press '!' or '?' to toggle between ability selection and description.
(Screen doesn't open if you have no abilities)

^ - religion screen
"Powers|Wrath"
(For atheists, screen doesn't open)

m - skills screen
"costs|targets"

M - spells
Describe|Hide|Show
(Doesn't open if you ave no spells)

Z/z - cast spell
to toggle spell view.

inventory/drop/wear/wield/take off/put on/remove
(difficult to tell)
